Hey, welcome to my site!

I’m Lukas, an outdoor guide and instructor with over a decade of experience in alpine and water sports. As a Level 4 ISIA Ski Instructor, I’ve spent the last 12 years teaching everything from kids to carving and park skiing. Plus, with 3 years as a instructor Trainer as well as a Freeride guide I love taking people off-piste to explore untouched terrain.

With a strong background in safety, having completed the IFMGA Winter Aspirant Test in Austria, I bring a high level of expertise to my guiding work. My rafting experience spans 9 years as a freelance raft guide, certified with IRS WI3 and VAO credentials. Also holder of a Svalbard weapons burrowing license.

Whether it’s skiing, freeriding, rafting, or just exploring the mountains, I’m always up for an adventure. Let’s get out there and make it happen!
